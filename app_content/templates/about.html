What is this about?

'Sample' broadcasts scientific news articles in a variety of fields. The website separates articles into 5 domains of knowledge: Space, Nature, Physiology, Sociology and Crypto. If a user wants to digress into a specific knowledge domain, they can access article tags to further delve into an area of interest. The website aims to target our need to constantly pursue information in a social-media-world. However, instead of mindlessly scrolling through celebrity feeds or depressing influencers, users can entertain themselves with knowledge.
